Welcome to Greece

Explore iconic landmarks and traditional foods

Traditional Greek Foods

Moussaka

A layered eggplant and minced meat dish, topped with creamy béchamel sauce.

Souvlaki

Grilled meat skewers served with pita, tzatziki, and veggies.

Tzatziki

A refreshing yogurt dip made with cucumber and garlic.

Famous Greek Landmarks

Acropolis & Parthenon

Ancient citadel and temple in Athens dedicated to goddess Athena.

Oia Village, Santorini

Famous for whitewashed homes, blue domes, and stunning sunsets.

Meteora Monasteries

UNESCO-listed monasteries perched on towering rock formations.

Welcome to Italy

Explore iconic landmarks and traditional foods

Traditional Italian Foods

Pizza

Classic Neapolitan pizza with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. Wood-fired to perfection.

Pasta Carbonara

Spaghetti tossed with eggs, pancetta, cheese, and pepper. A rich Roman favorite.

Arancini

Golden, crispy rice balls filled with meat ragù, peas, and melted mozzarella.

Famous Italian Landmarks

The Colosseum, Rome

An iconic amphitheater where gladiators once battled. A marvel of Roman engineering.

The Leaning Tower of Pisa

This famous tilted tower defies gravity and is a symbol of Italian uniqueness.

The Pantheon, Rome

An ancient temple with a massive dome and oculus, now a church filled with light.

Welcome to Turkey

Explore iconic landmarks and traditional foods

Traditional Turkish Foods

Lahmacun

A flatbread, often called Turkish pizza, topped with minced meat, tomato paste, garlic, and spices.

Doner Kebab

A popular dish made from stacked layers of lamb or beef, served in crusty bread with salad and sauce.

Baklava

A sweet pastry with filo layers, ground nuts, and honey syrup. A perfect Turkish dessert.

Famous Turkish Landmarks

Topkapi Palace

Once the primary residence of the Ottoman sultans, a historic palace in Istanbul with stunning gardens and artifacts.

Blue Mosque

Known for its stunning blue tiles and six minarets, this mosque is one of the most famous landmarks in Istanbul.

Bosphorus River

A natural strait that connects the Black Sea to the Sea of Marmara, forming a boundary between Asia and Europe.

Welcome to Spain

Discover landmarks and delicious food!

Famous Spanish Landmarks

Casa Batlló

One of the most famous buildings in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí, known for its whimsical architecture.

Sagrada Familia

Designed by Antoni Gaudí, this is one of Spain's most iconic landmarks in Barcelona.

La Boqueria

A popular market in Barcelona known for its fresh produce, meat, seafood, and local delicacies.

Spanish Cuisine

Paella

A famous rice dish from Valencia, usually made with seafood or chicken and served with saffron.

Patatas Bravas

Fried potato cubes served with a spicy tomato sauce, a staple tapas dish in Spain.

Spanish Pisto

A vegetable stew made with tomatoes, peppers, zucchini, and sometimes eggs, often served with bread.

Welcome to Switzerland

Discover the beauty of the Alps

Swiss Landmarks and Nature

The Swiss Alps

Majestic mountain range offering incredible skiing, hiking, and breathtaking views.

Lake Geneva

A stunning alpine lake surrounded by beautiful vineyards and mountain scenery.

Chillon Castle

A medieval fortress on the shores of Lake Geneva with a history spanning over 1000 years.
